Cat Detective & Missing Ruby

Introduction

Night, an ancient manor shrouded in moonlight, appearing mysterious and solemn. The manor's owner is a wealthy merchant, one of whose prides is a priceless ruby, kept in a luxurious exhibition room.
One morning, the maid discovers the ruby has vanished, throwing the entire manor into panic.

Character

The Butler

A loyal and meticulous old gentleman, responsible for managing the manor.

The Maid

Young and kind, but somewhat timid, responsible for cleaning and shopping.

Master Robert

The manor's owner with a serious personality.

The Detective Cat

A clever and alert cat who has solved many cases worldwide.
