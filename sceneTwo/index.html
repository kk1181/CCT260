The Exhibition

Clue 1

A faint scent of men's cologne lingers around the display, a fragrance that normally wouldn't be present in the exhibition room.
This scent matches the butler's cologne, showing that he had recently entered the room and stayed for some time.

Clue 2

A single light brown hair rests quietly on the floor.
This hair matches the maid's hair color.The maid is usually responsible for cleaning and organizing, but the exhibition room is personally overseen by the master, meaning she had no reason to leave traces here.
